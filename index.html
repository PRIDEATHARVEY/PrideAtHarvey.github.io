About Pride @ Harvey

The club is a LGBTQ+ group, a community of queer folk to discuss certain topics, socialise and have fun! Since 2022, it has been proud to support the LGBTQ+ students at the Harvey Grammar School, while providing a safe environment.

Furthermore, the club has had big milestones since its introduction. There has been many talkers and activists who visited the club and provided enjoyable presentations to the members about their personal expierences and their thoughts on certain matters. The club also recieved a board infront of the School's Diner where it frequently gets updated with new posters and infromational graphics about famous LGBTQ+ people.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is in charge of the club?
The staff who governs the club is Mrs Muckett, and the student representative of the club is Louis Bayley
What happens at Pride @ Harvey?
Community discussions, group games and interesting presentations
I am worried of coming to club incase I get outed
Simply put, what happens at club stays at club. We are very mindful as a club to respect eachother's privacy about their identity outside of Pride @ Harvey, people in the past have been able to use their preferred name within club and not outside to better suit their personal safety.
Any drinks?
Tea and Squash is always catered for!
