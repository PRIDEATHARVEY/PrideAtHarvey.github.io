Pride @ Harvey

About Pride @ Harvey

Harvey Pride is a LGBTQ+ group, who discuss certain topics, socialise and have fun! Since 2022 Harvey Pride has been proud to support the LGBTQ+ students of the Harvey Grammar School, providing them with a safe environment to thrive.

Furthermore, the club has reached a number of milestones since its introduction. There have been many talkers and activists who have visited the club and provided enjoyable presentations to the members about their personal experiences and their thoughts on certain matters. The club also received a notice board in front of the school's diner which frequently gets updated with new posters and informational graphics about famous LGBTQ+ people.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is in charge of the club?
The staff member who governs the club is Mrs Muckett, and the student representative of the club is Louis Bayley.
What happens at Pride @ Harvey?
Community discussions, group games and interesting presentations.
I am worried about coming to the club in case I get outed
We are very mindful as a club to respect each other's privacy and their identity outside of Pride @ Harvey. People in the past have been able to use their preferred name within club and not outside to better suit their personal safety.
Any drinks?
Tea and squash is always catered for!
